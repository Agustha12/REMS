Help & Support Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for properties?

Use the search bar on the home page to enter your desired location. You can also filter by property type and budget using the dropdown menus.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, UPI, and bank transfers. Payment details will be provided during the booking process.

How do I schedule a property viewing?

After logging in, click on the "View Details" button of any property and use the "Schedule Viewing" option to book a time slot.

What documents do I need to rent a property?

You'll need to provide: ID proof, address proof, income proof, and a security deposit as per the property requirements.
